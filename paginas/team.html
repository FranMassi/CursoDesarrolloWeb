GAME OVER is an indie games developer in Argentina.

Our mission is to build crazy, exciting worlds for you to explore and conquer.

The team

Marian Gibson

Marian is a Co-director of GAME OVER. She previously ran the animation studio Mechanical Apple and has worked on countless projects across film, games and music videos. Marian's been modding games from before computers were colour and has loved them since her first ever game rental: Faxanadu for NES.

Darren Korb

William is a Co-director of GAME OVER. He's been designing and building games for years, creating crazy worlds for people to poke around in and explore. He's loved games ever since his Dad found the wing boots for him in Zelda 2, and wants to recreate that experience of adventure and excitement for other people.

Nikola Sobajic
